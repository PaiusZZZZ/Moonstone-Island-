EXPLORATION UNENDING

You’ve fled your home, only to find yourself lost on an unknown island with a damaged boat. Your only option is to beam down to the forest below and gather the resources you need to repair your boat and set off to explore the vast, infinite horizon…

In Moonstone Island, you create your own story - there’s no wrong way to play! You may choose to save save by escaping the island, or you may wish to do a heroic journey on different shores.

BUILD YOUR WORLD

Settle down and farm the land, become an true survivor, hop from biome to biome collecting different materials, or delve into dangerous dungeons and lay claim to extraordinary treasures.

Discover ancient temples and a lot of different biomes!. Make use of hundreds of materials and over two thousand objects to build a sleepy secluded cabin in the woods, a medieval castle, or an underwater arcade.

MULTIPLAYER + MODDABLE

Moonstone Island has been built from the ground up to be multiplayer and easily moddable. You have the tools to make the world your own and modify the game to suit your play style - add new npcs, biomes, dungeons, and quests - the possibilities are limitless.
